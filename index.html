Relativity is built on two core postulates.

1: Equivalence of Physical Laws

The laws of physics are the same in all internal frames of reference.

2: Constancy of the Speed of Light

The speed of light in a vacuum, c = 3.00 x 10^8 m/s, is the same in all internal frames of reference, regardless of the motion of the source or the observer of the light.
